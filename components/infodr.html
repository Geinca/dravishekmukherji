About   Dr. Avishek Mukherjee

Dr. Avishek Mukherjee is one of the foremost practising urosurgeons in eastern India. He is trained from two of the most prestigious medical universities of India - AIIMS New Delhi and PGI Chandigarh.

After completing his training, he came to the city of joy Kolkata in 2003. In Kolkata, he joined the esteemed team at Wockhardt Hospital and Kidney Institute from 2003 till 2009.

Dr Mukherjee has received his medical and surgical training from the most respected institutes of this country. He joined MBBS at the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) at New Delhi in the year 1991. After completing his basic medical training he immediately joined the surgical training programme also at AIIMS and completed his Masters in Surgery in the year 2000.
