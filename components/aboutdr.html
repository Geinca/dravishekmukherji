About NKPC Kidney Clinic

NKPC kidney clinic was established in the year 2007. For the last seventeen years we have provided state of the art kidney care. We provide the full spectrum of kidney, bladder, and prostate surgery, nephrology services and kidney transplantation too.

We have a speciality surgical center of 26 beds located on the E M Bypass, equipped with the latest technology and staffed by experienced specialists dedicated to your kidney health.
