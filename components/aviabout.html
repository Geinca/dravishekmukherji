About Dr. Avishek Mukherjee

Dr. Avishek Mukherjee is one of the foremost practicing urosurgeons in Eastern India, trained from two of the most prestigious medical universities in the country. With over two decades of experience, he has pioneered numerous advanced urological procedures in the region, making world-class urosurgical care accessible to patients across all economic backgrounds.

After completing his training at AIIMS New Delhi and PGI Chandigarh, Dr. Mukherjee came to Kolkata in 2003 where he worked with renowned urosurgeons at Wockhardt Hospital and Kidney Institute. His commitment to affordable, high-quality care led him to establish NKPC Kidney Clinic in 2007, which has since grown into a super-specialized kidney hospital providing comprehensive urological care.
