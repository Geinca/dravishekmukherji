About Dr. Avishek

Dr. Avishek Mukherjee is one of the foremost practising urosurgeons in Eastern India. Trained at two of India's most prestigious medical institutes – AIIMS New Delhi and PGI Chandigarh – he brings over two decades of expertise in urological care with a rare blend of clinical excellence, innovation, and patient compassion.

After completing his MCh in Urosurgery at PGI Chandigarh in 2003, Dr. Avishek moved to Kolkata where he worked at Wockhardt Hospital and Kidney Institute alongside legendary surgeons. His journey reflects a commitment to bringing world-class urological care to patients across all economic backgrounds.

Laparoscopic Reconstructive Surgery

Laparoscopic Pyeloplasty

When the kidney cannot drain into the ureter efficiently due to congenital kidney ureter obstruction, we reconstruct the ureter with complete laparoscopic reconstruction.

Laparoscopic Ureter Reimplantation

When the ureter is damaged or forms a constriction resulting in obstruction of the kidney, we use a bladder flap to save the kidney and replace the ureter by laparoscopy.

Laparoscopic Vesicovaginal Fistula Repair

When accidentally the bladder perforates into the vagina causing urine leakage, we use laparoscopy to repair the bladder and save the patient from severe morbidity.
